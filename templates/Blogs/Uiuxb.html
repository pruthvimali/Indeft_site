What is UI/UX design?

UI or user interface of a mobile app determines its look and feel. UI decides the visual appearance of the application, the placement of graphics, texts, and buttons, its navigation, and so on. A UI designer primarily focuses on the end-users needs when developing a mobile application's UI.

On the contrary, UX or user experience defines users' impressions of the UI and the interactions they forge with it. UX plays an instrumental role in deciphering customer engagement, trust, and satisfaction. Factors like usability, credibility, and accessibility impact the UX of a mobile app. The more user-friendly, functional, and effective your mobile app design is, the better its UX.
