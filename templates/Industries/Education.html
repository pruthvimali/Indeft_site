Our custom education and eLearning development services

Test Assessment Engine

Our advanced assessment engines utilize AI algorithms and machine learning techniques to automate grading, generate detailed performance analytics, and offer adaptive assessments, ensuring accurate and efficient evaluation of students' knowledge and progress.

Course Digitization

We transform traditional educational content into digital formats, leveraging OCR, document digitization, and CMS technologies. This enables seamless access, distribution, and customization of educational materials across multiple devices, enhancing the learning experience.

Learning Management Systems (LMS)

Our robust LMS platforms feature comprehensive functionalities such as content authoring, curriculum management, learner analytics, and social collaboration tools. This facilitates seamless online course delivery, progress tracking, and effective communication between educators and students.

E-Learning Portals

We develop interactive e-learning portals with engaging user interfaces, multimedia content integration, and personalized learning paths. Leveraging Learning Experience Platforms (LXP), gamification, and VR/AR technologies, we create immersive educational experiences that captivate learners.

Online Examination Portals

Our secure and scalable online examination portals support features like proctoring, timed assessments, question randomization, and real-time result generation. Employing secure browsers, facial recognition, and plagiarism detection algorithms, we ensure the integrity of online examinations.

Virtual Event Portals

We create immersive virtual event platforms with features like customizable virtual environments, attendee registration and management, live streaming, video conferencing, and interactive chat functionalities and real-time analytics.

Measurement & Analytics

We leverage key performance indicators (KPIs), learning management system (LMS) data, and business intelligence platforms to provide actionable insights, measure training impact, and optimize learning strategies.

IT Managed Services

We offer proactive monitoring, patch management, and network security services using technologies like intrusion detection systems (IDS), firewalls, and antivirus software to ensure smooth operation of systems and apps via performance optimization, data backups, and disaster recovery solutions.
